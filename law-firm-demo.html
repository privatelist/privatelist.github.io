How It Works: 5-Step Workflow

The system orchestrates multiple data sources into a single coherent briefing through intelligent automation.

1

Trigger (7:30 AM)

Cron trigger fires automatically every morning at 7:30 AM. Or test manually anytime with the Manual Trigger node.

2

Gather Data

Simultaneously fetch calendar events, new leads, outstanding tasks, and invoices from your systems. Uses sample data in demo; connects to real systems in production.

3

Generate AI Summary

YOUR AI Agent generates a natural-language executive summary. Reads like your trusted advisor, not a chatbot.

4

Format & Send Email

HTML email formatted with your firm's branding. Calendar, leads, tasks, and cash flow sections all included.

5

Optional: Slack/Teams

Optionally post summary to Slack or Microsoft Teams for team-wide visibility.
