# Private List Consulting Brand Guide

## Colors

### Primary Palette

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Navy Blue** | `#1E3A5F` | rgb(30, 58, 95) | Primary brand color, headers, logo |
| **Copper** | `#C47D3A` | rgb(196, 125, 58) | Accent, CTAs, highlights |
| **Sage Green** | `#5A7E7E` | rgb(90, 126, 126) | Secondary, subtle accents |

### Supporting Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Dark Navy** | `#0F2841` | rgb(15, 40, 65) | Hover states, depth |
| **Copper Dark** | `#B36E2E` | rgb(179, 110, 46) | Hover accent |
| **Dark Gray** | `#2C2C2C` | rgb(44, 44, 44) | Body text |
| **Light Gray** | `#F7F8F8` | rgb(247, 248, 248) | Backgrounds |
| **Border Gray** | `#E8EAEA` | rgb(232, 234, 234) | Subtle borders |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | rgb(255, 255, 255) | Clean backgrounds |

### Color Combinations

**Primary CTA:** Copper `#C47D3A` button with White `#FFFFFF` text
**Secondary CTA:** Sage `#5A7E7E` outline with Sage text
**Headers:** Navy `#1E3A5F`
**Body:** Dark Gray `#2C2C2C`
**Backgrounds:** White or Light Gray `#F7F8F8`

## Brand Voice

- Direct, no fluff
- Competent and quiet
- Results over promises
- Enterprise-grade, not flashy

## Tagline Ideas

- "AI that delivers intelligence, not demos"
- "The AI team member you've been waiting for"
- "Quiet. Competent. Results."
