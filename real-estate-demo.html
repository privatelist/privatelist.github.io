Technical Integrations

📋 Jotform

Lead capture form with property type, budget, location, timeline fields. Mobile-optimized for mobile shoppers.

⚙️ Zapier

Automation orchestration. Watches form submissions, triggers chatbot, logs data, manages integrations.

🤖 Lindy Chatbot

AI-powered qualification. 4 targeted questions. Warm, conversational tone. Completes in ~90 seconds.

📊 Scoring Engine

Behavioral scoring based on responses. Rates leads HOT/WARM/COLD. Considers timeline, budget fit, competition.

💼 Follow Up Boss / HubSpot

CRM integration. Automatically creates contact, assigns to agent, flags temperature, adds to pipeline.

📅 Google Calendar

Auto-schedules 15-min intro calls. Sends SMS + email confirmations to agent and lead.

Key Features

🤖 AI Agent Qualification

Q1: Timeline & Urgency

"Are you looking to buy within the next 30, 60, or 90 days? Or are you still in the research phase?"

Identifies HOT (30 days) vs. WARM (60 days) vs. COLD (research)

Q2: Existing Representation

"Are you currently working with another real estate agent?"

Fresh leads score higher. Existing representation = referral opportunity.

Q3: Budget Alignment

"What's your comfortable price range?"

Matches to agent specialization. Outside range = refer or nurture.

Q4: Geographic Routing

"Which neighborhoods interest you most?"

Routes to territory specialist. Assigns the right agent instantly.

📊 Behavioral Scoring System

Each response scores points. Final score (0-100+) determines urgency and routing. Higher score = faster response, higher priority.

90+
HOT LEAD
30-day timeline, clear intent, perfect geographic & budget fit. No competing agent.

→ PRIORITY routing (0-15 min SLA)

60-89
WARM LEAD
60-90 day timeline, good intent, partial geographic/budget alignment, exploring options.

→ Standard routing (2-4 hour SLA)

40-59
LUKEWARM
90+ day timeline, research phase, exploring multiple areas or price points.

→ Nurture sequence (auto-follow-ups)

🎯 Intelligent Routing Logic

The system doesn't just score leads—it matches them to the best agent based on:

  • ✅ Geographic territory: Agent specializes in lead's target neighborhood
  • ✅ Price range expertise: Agent typically sells in lead's budget
  • ✅ Current availability: Agent has capacity (max 5 active leads)
  • ✅ Response history: Some agents are faster responders
  • ✅ Lead type match: Buyer agents for buyers, investor specialists for investors
